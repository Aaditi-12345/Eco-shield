Our Process

How We Clean Your Furniture

Our comprehensive 4-step cleaning process ensures the best results for all fabric types.

1

Fabric Analysis

We identify your fabric type (cotton, linen, microfibre, leather, silk, or delicate) to determine the safest cleaning method.

2

Pre-Treatment

Specialized solutions are applied to break down dirt, stains, and oils before the main cleaning process begins.

3

Deep Cleaning

Hot water extraction removes embedded dirt, allergens, and bacteria from deep within the fabric fibers.

4

Protection

We apply protective treatments including Scotchgard to guard against future stains and extend furniture life.
