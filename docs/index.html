We provide a wide range of Professional Carpet Cleaning and Stain Elimination Services, complemented by Upholstery, Tile, Pest Control, Water Removal, and End-of-Lease Cleaning Services on the Gold Coast.

We primarily use two methods for carpet cleaning: hot water extraction and dry cleaning. Our hot water extraction method, often referred to as steam cleaning, involves using high-pressure hot water to agitate the carpet fibers and dissolve dirt in the carpet. For more delicate carpets, we use a dry cleaning method which requires minimal moisture and prevents shrinkage and color running. Both methods utilize eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for both pets and children.

We recommend professional carpet cleaning at least once a year to maintain the quality and hygiene of your carpets. Regular cleaning helps to remove accumulated dirt, allergens, and microorganisms that regular vacuuming cannot. For homes with pets, children, or high foot traffic, we suggest increasing the frequency to every 6 months to ensure your carpets remain fresh, clean, and free of allergens and odors.
